this is the pitch deck and as you can see the founder flow doesn't align properly.

the founder flow is that,
- the input their problem/solution
- next screen is them selecting an industry their solution is in or the type manually if not on the list
- next screen, they select the stage they are(idea stage, prototype, MVP, Growth ) and click generate to start building the team archtecture.
- next screen shows them two options, Max Growth and Lean Team.
- after selecting a team structure, theFoundry present the blue print. let's say the selected lean team, theFoundry will show them 2 or 3 roles depending on their solution/product. At this point, the founder has the option to customize the role, for instance replace a fullstack/ai engineer with fullstack/smart contract engineer. the founder can even decided to add more roles.
after the founder is satisfied with the roles, they save and move to the next screen,
- this screen populates the architecture/the blue print/the roles with talents from 3mtt. each role will be filled with a talent. There will be list of other available qualified talents for those roles so that the founder can also at this stage customize the talent by replace a talent for a role. because the first talent the founder selects might not agree to work with the founder and so the founder will need to come back to this screen and select a differnt talent. 
- the next screen will be to meet with the talent. immediately the founder is satisfied and locks in the architecure and the selected team, the talents will receive notification of a require for hire. The talent can accept or reject. If the talent rejects, the founder will be notified and shows as rejected for that founder so that a founder doesn't mistakenly try to reachout to the same candidate more than once on the same project. If the talent accepts, the talent can start a chat or schedule a call. The founder will be given the option to start a chat with the talents he selects.
- if everything goes well, and both sides agrees, then the founder can now finish the team building process and save the progress. 


you can make every screen to be in form of tabs so that the founder can go back and modify anything the want.
