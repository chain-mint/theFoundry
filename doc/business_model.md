Disruptive Business Model: TheFoundry Venture Architecture
1. The Core Disruption: Architecture-as-a-Service (AaaS)
In 2026, the traditional recruitment model (charging 15–25% of salary) is failing early-stage startups that lack consistent cash flow. TheFoundry disrupts this by replacing "Hiring" with "AI-powered Venture Architecture with integrated talent matchmaking"
The "Lean Team" Algorithm: Using AI to group 5–6 traditional roles into 2–3 "Multi-Role" positions filled by 3MTT Fellows.
Outcome-Linked Pricing: We eliminate upfront hiring fees. Instead, our revenue is triggered by the iDICE Milestone Disbursement.
Talent Equity Management: We facilitate Equity-as-a-Service (EaaS), managing a 5–10% equity pool for the "Founding Engineers". This turns employees into owners, which is a major trend for 2026 retention.

TheFoundry isn't just a tech platform; it's a Regulatory Maximizer. We help founders unlock the benefits of the Nigeria Startup Act 2026.
